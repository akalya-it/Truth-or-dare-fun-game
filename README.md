**Truth or Dare Game**

A fun, web-based Truth or Dare game for multiple players. Designed for friends and family with safe and playful questions.

**Features**

Supports 8 players (or more)

Truth and Dare options

Another button to generate a new question for the same player

Skip button to move to the next player

50 fun Truths & 50 playful Dares

Manual turns â€” player clicks to play

**How to Play**

Open index.html in a browser.

See which player's turn it is.

Click Truth or Dare to get a question.

Click Another if you want a new question.

Click Skip to move to the next player.
